g chiarion is a London-based artist and researcher. She was born and raised in Italy by a British mother and Italian father. She moved to London at 19 to study and has remained here ever since. In her work she explores the ways in which people interact with spaces and landscapes. She is interested in the stories that places hold for individuals and communities.
